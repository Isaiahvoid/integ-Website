From "Hello World" to High Hopes

The Two Paths of Programming

What if sinabi sa’yo na ang course na pinili mo ay hindi pala ganun kasimple tulad ng iniisip mo noon? Akala ko dati na kapag IT/CS, malaki ang sweldo at madali lang yung course—parang puro typing at computer lang. Noong una, kampante ako kasi iniisip ko sapat na yung hilig ko sa technology at basic computer skills ko para kayanin ito. Pero habang tumatagal sa college, doon ko narealize na marami pala akong hindi alam at mas marami pa akong kailangang matutunan—mga challenges na hindi mo talaga maiintindihan hangga’t hindi mo pa nararanasan. Sa blog na ito, ibabahagi ko yung experiences ko, struggles, at lessons na sana mas maaga kong nalaman bago pumasok sa IT/CS.

The Reality Check I Didn't Expect

When simple logic starts feeling like a maze

I remember during my first year, the very first code I wrote was the simplest one, "Hello World." After that, may seatwork na pinagawa yung prof, and nagawa ko naman siya. Natuwa ako sa sarili ko at naisip ko, maybe kaya ko pala 'to. Pero alam ko rin na umpisa pa lang iyon. Habang tumatagal, lalo pang humihirap ang mga coding problems at nagiging mas komplikado ang logic. There were times na sobrang frustrated ako kasi hindi ko maintindihan agad yung problem at kung paano gagawan ng solution. May mga pagkakataon talaga na blanko yung utak ko, parang hindi ko alam kung saan magsisimula. Dahil doon, I tried asking help from AI. At first, it really helped me, binigyan ako ng idea kung paano i-solve yung problem at inexplain yung logic behind the code. However, habang tumatagal, napansin ko na naging kampante ako. Instead of fully understanding the problem, minsan binabasa ko lang yung sagot without really learning it. Doon ko narealize na kung palagi akong aasa, hindi talaga ako mag-iimprove. That experience taught me that AI can be helpful, but only if I use it the right way, to understand, not just to depend on it.

Lessons from the Compilation Error

Error as a Stepping Stone

Looking back, narealize ko na ang IT/CS ay hindi lang tungkol sa hilig sa technology at pagiging marunong gumamit ng computer. Hindi pala sapat ang interest lang, dahil kailangan din ng tiyaga, practice, at tamang mindset para makasabay sa mga lessons. Maraming beses akong nahirapan at nagkamali, lalo na noong umasa ako masyado sa AI instead na intindihin ang logic sa likod ng code. Natutunan ko na ang AI ay malaking tulong, pero mali kung gagamitin ito bilang shortcut at kapalit ng sariling effort. For those who are just starting in IT/CS, my advice is not to be afraid of struggling, not to compare yourself to others, and use AI properly as a tool for learning, not just a way to avoid putting in effort. Moving forward, I plan to practice regularly, understand the concepts step by step, and use AI only as a guide. I now understand that failure is a part of progress, and every mistakes helps me learn and improve.
